 # Global Unicorn Startup Ecosystem & Valuation Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the global unicorn startup ecosystem to identify trends in startup growth, valuation, funding, industry performance, and investor activity. The analysis is based on data from 1,037 unicorn companies across 46 countries and 33 industries.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the unicorn landscape, helping stakeholders understand market trends, investment patterns, and the factors driving startup success.

## Objectives

- Analyze global unicorn distribution across countries and industries.
- Evaluate valuation and funding trends.
- Identify leading investors and investment patterns.
- Examine unicorn growth over time.
- Generate insights for strategic business and investment decisions.

## Tools & Technologies

- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query
- Pivot Tables
- Dashboarding & Data Visualization

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Unicorn Companies
- Total Valuation
- Total Countries
- Total Industries
- Total Investors

## Analysis Performed

- KPI Monitoring
- Unicorn Growth Trend Analysis
- Country-wise Analysis
- Industry-wise Analysis
- Funding vs Valuation Analysis
- Top Unicorn Companies Analysis
- Investor Analysis

## Key Insights

- The United States leads in unicorn company creation and valuation.
- FinTech is among the leading unicorn industries.
- Unicorn creation accelerated significantly in recent years.
- Funding has a strong influence on startup valuation.
- Leading investors play a critical role in unicorn growth.

## Business Value

- Helps identify high-growth startup opportunities.
- Provides insights into global investment trends.
- Supports data-driven business and investment decisions.
- Enables analysis of leading industries and markets.
- Assists stakeholders in understanding the unicorn ecosystem.

## Conclusion

- The global unicorn ecosystem continues to grow rapidly.
- Startup valuation is strongly influenced by funding and investor support.
- Technology-driven industries dominate the unicorn landscape.
- The United States remains a leader in unicorn creation.
- Data-driven analysis helps uncover valuable business and investment insights.
